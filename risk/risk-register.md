# Risk Register

## Purpose
Centralized log of identified risks across product, tech, security, and compliance.

---

## Risk Levels
- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

---

## Risk Template

| ID | Description | Category | Severity | Mitigation | Status |
|----|------------|----------|----------|------------|--------|

---

## Rules
- High/Critical risks auto-trigger Council Debate
- Risks must have owners
- Closed risks require evidence

## Risk Lifecycle
Each risk MUST include:
- Owner
- Status (Open / Mitigated / Accepted)
- Review date
- Council decision reference (if accepted)
