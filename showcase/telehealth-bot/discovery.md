# Discovery Output

## Discovery Summary

HealthAI Telehealth is a mobile application designed to provide telehealth services to patients, especially those in remote areas or unable to leave their homes. Using an AI triage system, the app will assess symptoms reported by users and prioritize them based on the severity of their condition. This will aid healthcare providers in efficiently managing their patients. The app's features include user registration, symptom reporting, AI triage, telehealth appointments via video call, secure messaging, and HIPAA-compliant data storage and transmission.

## Assumptions

- The team has the necessary skills and knowledge to develop a robust AI triage system.
- The target users have access to a reliable internet connection and devices capable of running the app.
- The app will be primarily used by patients and healthcare providers, and the cost will be covered either by the patients or their insurance providers.
- The app will be available to users in multiple regions, including the European Union.

## Functional Requirements

1. User registration and secure login.
2. An interface for users to report their symptoms.
3. An AI triage system to assess and prioritize reported symptoms.
4. Video call functionality for telehealth appointments.
5. Secure messaging system for patient-provider communication.
6. HIPAA-compliant data storage and transmission.

## Non-Functional Requirements

### Performance
- The app should load quickly and operate smoothly, providing a positive user experience.
- The AI triage system must provide accurate assessments in real-time.

### Scalability
- The app should be able to handle an increasing number of users without performance degradation.

### Availability
- The app should be available 24/7, with minimal downtime for maintenance or updates.

### Security
- The app must implement strong security measures to protect sensitive health information.

## Compliance Profile

- Applicable regulations: HIPAA, GDPR
- Data sensitivity classification: Personal Health Information (PHI)
- Audit requirements: Regular audits to ensure compliance with HIPAA and GDPR

## Open Questions

- What are the specifics of the AI triage system? What technology and algorithms will be used?
- How will the app handle emergency situations where immediate medical attention is needed?
- How will the app manage consent from users for data collection and processing?

## Council Flags

- The technical challenge of building a robust AI triage system.
- Ensuring a smooth user experience while complying with healthcare regulations.
- Security and privacy risks associated with handling sensitive health information.
- Compliance with data residency laws if the app operates across different regions or countries.
