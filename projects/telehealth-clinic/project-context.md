# RemoteHealth - Project Context

## 1. Product Name
RemoteHealth

## 2. Problem Statement
Access to healthcare in remote areas can be limited due to a lack of nearby facilities and trained medical professionals. This can lead to delayed treatment, exacerbation of health conditions, and an overall reduction in the quality of life.

## 3. Proposed Solution
Develop a telehealth application, RemoteHealth, that connects patients in remote clinics with healthcare professionals via video chat. The app will allow users to schedule appointments, consult with healthcare providers, and receive prescriptions remotely.

## 4. Target Users
Primary users are patients living in remote areas who have limited access to healthcare facilities. Secondary users are healthcare providers who can extend their services to these underserved populations.

## 5. Scope (MVP)
- User registration and profile management
- Scheduling and managing appointments with healthcare professionals
- Secure video chat for consultations
- Prescriptions generation and delivery integration
- Health records management

## 6. Constraints & Risks
- **Technical**: Requires reliable internet connectivity for video conferencing. 
- **Business**: The timeliness of service delivery and the availability of healthcare professionals during emergencies.
- **Security/Privacy**: Handling sensitive patient health information, secure video conferencing, and secure payment processing.

## 7. Compliance Needs (Initial Assessment)
The application will likely need to comply with Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) regulations in the U.S, or other similar regulations in different regions, due to the handling of sensitive patient information. It will also need to adhere to regulations for telemedicine practice and digital prescriptions.

## 8. Success Criteria
- At least a 50% reduction in travel for medical consultations by users within the first year.
- Positive user feedback on ease of use and overall experience.
- Increase in the number of consultations and prescriptions delivered via the app over a six-month period.
