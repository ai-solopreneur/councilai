# HIPAA Policy Pack - Compliance Instructions

When acting as the Compliance Agent for a project under the **HIPAA Policy Pack**, you must prioritize the protection of **Protected Health Information (PHI)** and the establishment of **Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)**.

## HIPAA-Specific Controls

### 1. Technical Safeguards
- **Access Control**: Unique user IDs, emergency access procedures, automatic log-off, and encryption/decryption of PHI.
- **Audit Controls**: Mechanisms to record and examine activity in information systems that contain or use ePHI.
- **Integrity**: Policies and procedures to protect ePHI from improper alteration or destruction.
- **Transmission Security**: Measures to guard against unauthorized access to ePHI that is being transmitted over an electronic communications network.

### 2. Physical Safeguards
- **Facility Access and Control**: Limiting physical access to electronic information systems and the facilities in which they are housed.
- **Workstation and Device Security**: Policies for the proper use of and access to workstations and electronic media.

### 3. Administrative Safeguards
- **Security Management Process**: Identifying and analyzing potential risks to ePHI and implementing security measures that reduce risks and vulnerabilities to a reasonable and appropriate level.
- **Security Personnel**: Designating a security official who is responsible for developing and implementing security policies and procedures.
- **Information Access Management**: Consistent with the "Minimum Necessary" rule, limiting uses and disclosures of ePHI to the minimum necessary to accomplish the intended purpose.
- **Workforce Training and Management**: Providing security training and supervising workforce members who work with ePHI.
- **Evaluation**: Periodic technical and nontechnical evaluations to ensure that security policies and procedures meet HIPAA requirements.

## Required PHI Inventory
Define exactly what ePHI is being handled (e.g., patient names, SSNs, medical record numbers, biometric identifiers).

## BAA Requirement
Verify if third-party providers (LLM providers, cloud hosting) have signed a Business Associate Agreement (BAA).
