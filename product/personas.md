# CouncilAI User Personas

## 1. Solo Founder (AI-Native)
Goals:
- Build production-grade systems safely
- Avoid architectural and compliance mistakes
- Move fast without breaking trust

Pain Points:
- Over-reliance on AI hallucinations
- No governance structure
- Hard to scale quality

CouncilAI Value:
- Acts as a virtual senior engineering council

---

## 2. AI Startup Team
Goals:
- Ship fast with multiple AI tools
- Maintain consistency across agents
- Prepare for audits and enterprise deals

Pain Points:
- Agent chaos
- Inconsistent decisions
- Missing documentation

CouncilAI Value:
- Standardizes AI behavior across the SDLC

---

## 3. Regulated Startup
Goals:
- Meet compliance early
- Reduce regulatory risk
- Be audit-ready

Pain Points:
- Compliance added too late
- No traceability
- High rework costs

CouncilAI Value:
- Compliance-first SDLC by design

---

## 4. Enterprise Innovation Team
Goals:
- Experiment safely
- Control risk
- Maintain accountability

Pain Points:
- Shadow AI usage
- Lack of governance
- Security concerns

CouncilAI Value:
- Safe sandbox for AI-powered development
