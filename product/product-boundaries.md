# CouncilAI – Product Boundaries

## What CouncilAI Is
- A governed AI SDLC operating system
- A protocol-first framework
- A compliance-aware agent orchestration model

## What CouncilAI Is NOT
- A coding assistant
- A low-code platform
- An autonomous software factory
- A replacement for engineers or architects

## Design Philosophy
CouncilAI optimizes for:
- Safety over speed
- Auditability over convenience
- Governance over autonomy
- Long-term trust over short-term output
