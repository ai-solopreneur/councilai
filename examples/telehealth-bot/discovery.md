# Discovery – Telehealth Triage Bot

## Problem
Patients utilize emergency services for minor issues, or ignore critical symptoms due to lack of immediate guidance.
A 24/7 AI triage bot can route patients to the correct level of care (Self-care, Urgency Care, ER).

## Target Users
- Patients (Mobile App)
- Nurse Practitioners (Dashboard)

## Goals
- Reduce unnecessary ER visits by 20%
- < 1 minute time-to-triage
- 100% HIPAA compliance

## Constraints
- No medical diagnosis (Triage only)
- Data residency: US Only
- E2EE for all chat data

## Compliance & Risks
- **HIPAA**: PHI handling is core functionality.
- **Liability**: Risk of incorrect triage leading to patient harm.
- **Security**: Zero-trust architecture required.
