# Discovery – Loyalty Program SaaS

## Problem
Businesses struggle to retain customers across channels.
Existing loyalty platforms are expensive, rigid, or poorly integrated.

## Target Users
- SMBs
- E-commerce brands
- Retail chains

## Goals
- Increase repeat purchases
- Unified rewards across channels
- Fast onboarding

## Constraints
- Multi-tenant SaaS
- GDPR compliant
- Scalable reward processing
